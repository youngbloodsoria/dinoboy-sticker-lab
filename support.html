Donate PTO.
Give Time.
Change Everything.

When a company allows employees to donate paid time off, that time can become real support for families in the middle of treatment. Time to be at the hospital. Time to be at home. Time to just be. Every hour can help a family breathe.

PTO donation programs require employer participation and legal/tax review before launch.

Learn More About PTO Donations ›

How It Works

  1. 1You or your company pledges PTO hours.
  2. 2We work with the employer to convert approved PTO into support.
  3. 3Families receive help for lost income, travel, housing, or care needs.
